The Last of Us

The Last of Us is a 2013 action-adventure survival horror game developed by Naughty Dog and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It was released for the PlayStation 3 on June 14, 2013, and was ported to PlayStation 4 in 2014 and PlayStation 5 in 2020.
